Vision

Building a people’s movement for inclusion, democracy, and sustainable life on land

Mission

Beginning with the vulnerable, empowering with law-based advocacy, and driving through village community institutions to create better waters, forests, and land.



About Us

We are a people’s movement. We feel proud to mention that our centre was built by voluntary labour of 15 villages. Every activity of ours is conceived by people’s needs and has full participation of people throughout implementation. It would be apt to write the names of about five thousand village volunteers here. But the limits of this medium do not allow that. So we salute our village volunteers here at the top of this page!
